Setting Up

1.) Go to OpenWeathers website at https://openweathermap.org/.
2.) Sign in to your account. if you do not have an account, create one.
3.) Go to the API Keys section of your account. You should be given a default key. Make sure your key is active!
4.) Copy and paste your key into the key input in the welcome page
5.) Add a city and find it's current weather!
